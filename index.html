How live data is refreshed

This tool combines two refresh speeds. Live market data (price, market capitalization, filed company fundamentals) refreshes automatically every 48 hours, or instantly when you press Refresh, via free, authorized public APIs. Research-baseline data (market sizing, growth assumptions) refreshes monthly via the same automated, in-site mechanism, drawing only on named, open, or explicitly licensed public sources.
